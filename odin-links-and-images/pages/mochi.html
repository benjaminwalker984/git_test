JPG Mochi

JPG images are great for keeping file size small, even with a variety of colors.

JPG images dont allow for transparent pixels, which is why you can see white edges on the images sometimes

Gif Mochi

Gif images have a limited color palette, which makes them bad for photos

What they lack in detail they make up for in being able to have simple animations

PNG Mochi

PNG's are great for a high level of detail. Think photos, logos, and Icons

Because of the high level of detail, they have larger file sizes than their JPG counterparts

SVG Mochi

SVG images are different from the ones above due to their scalable quality

Because they arent pixel based, they have many applications where quality is a concern.

One potential issue with PNG's is for them to display consistently across browsers, one must convert any text fields to outlines using your image editor
